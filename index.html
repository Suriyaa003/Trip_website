Experience the Serenity of Vellingiri Hills

The Vellingiri Hills, located in Tamil Nadu, India, are a famous trekking and pilgrimage destination known for their breathtaking landscapes and spiritual significance.

Things to Know:

  • Location: Near Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu
  • Best Time to Visit: November to May
  • Trek Distance: Approx. 15 km (One way)
  • Difficulty Level: Moderate to Difficult

Travel Route from Erode to Vellingiri Hills:

To reach Vellingiri Hills from Erode, you can travel via Coimbatore:

  • By Road: Erode to Coimbatore (100 km) via NH544, then Coimbatore to Poondi (Vellingiri Base) by local transport.
  • By Train: Take a train from Erode to Coimbatore Junction, then proceed to Poondi by bus or taxi.
  • By Bus: Direct and connecting buses are available from Erode to Coimbatore, and further to Poondi.
